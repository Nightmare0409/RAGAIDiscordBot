CAMPUS SWIPE SYSTEM: 
INDIVIDUAL SWIPE ACCESS: 
Our residence halls rely on two swipe systems: Blackboard, which is primarily run by the ID card 
office on campus and SiPass, which is primarily run by OUPD. Hamlin, Oak View, and Vandenberg 
operate on the Blackboard system, while Hillcrest runs on SiPass. The external doors for Van 
Wagoner operate on Blackboard, while the interior doors operate on SiPass. Additionally, all 
community key boxes operate on SiPass. During business hours, If you run into a student who is  
experiencing swipe/access issues for a building on the Blackboard system, you should direct the 
student to the Campus ID Card Office. If a student is experiencing these issues after hours, check 
the student out a department card, and advise the student to go to the Campus ID Card Office 
the following day.  
If a student is  experiencing swipe issues for Hillcrest or the Van Wagoner internal doors, during 
business hours, you should contact the OUPD IT Help desk to assist with getting their access 
turned back on. You can contact the OUPD Tech Desk at (248-370-3407). You can also call OUPD 
and request to be transferred to their IT Help Desk. If you are unable to get in touch with the IT 
desk, you should check the student out a department card, and email 
servicedesk@oupolice.com, notifying them that a student does not have access. Include the 
student’s name, ID card number, GID #, and email. This process is the same after hours.Please 
note that if staff members receive new ID cards, staff access must be re-added.
